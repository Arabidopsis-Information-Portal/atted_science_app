ATTED-II Website

This application displays coexpressed genes for a gene of interest. The display is in tabular format. The table in the “Coexpressed Genes” tab gets populated with coexpressed genes for the given gene identifier, one row per coexpressed gene. Each row includes a quantitative score, which can be the pre-computed Pearson Correlation coefficient (COR), or Mutual Rank (MR).

ATTED-II is a comprehensive database resource of coexpressed gene sets in Arabidopsis that provides coregulated gene relationships inferred from microarray data and cis elements. It includes lists and networks of coexpressed genes, and visual representation of expression patterns. The database can be used for a wide variety of experimental designs, including targeting of genes for functional identification or for regulatory investigation.

Instructions:

To get started, enter a gene identifier in the input field labeled “Locus”. An example of a valid locus ID provided in the search box. Once the search button is clicked the list of coexpressed genes.


Additional Features:

  • The input field labeled "Relationship Type" allows to choose the type of prediction score of the coexpressed genes. You may choose between Pearson Correlation Coefficient or Mutual Rank.
  • The list of coexpressed genes can be filtered by the degree of coexpression. The cut-off score for the degree of coexpression is entered in the field labeled "Treshold" .
  • In addition, the list of coexpressed genes can be searched by typing a keyword in the "Search" field right above the table of coexpressed genes.
